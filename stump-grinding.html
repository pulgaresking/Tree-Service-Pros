STUMP GRINDING


Stump Grinding

As a full service tree company, Tree Service Pros offers stump grinding as the fastest and least abrasive way to remove your stump. Letting Mother Nature gradually wear down the stump is one option though leaving the stump doesn’t allow you many options for restoring your landscape.

Removing your stump offers you the most choices as you can plant grass or other trees, flowers or shrubs where your stump used to be. You’ll enjoy having us do the work so all you need to do is make plans for the area.

Free estimates

As with all our services, we give no-obligation, free estimates so you’ll know exactly what it will cost to remove your stump before any work is done. Offering affordable stump grinding and for Meridian homeowners-no matter what your budget. Plus, your stump grinder expert will be scheduled whenever it’s convenient for you.

Grinding better than other options

There are other ways of getting rid of a stump though most involve speeding up the natural decay of the stump, which usually involves some sort of chemical treatment. These chemicals can be bad for your lawn (and the environment) and in the end, are still slower than having stump grinding done.

TREE REMOVAL


Professional tree removal services

Whether a tree needs to be removed because it’s a hazard, dead, diseased, causing an obstruction or you just want to have it taken away for aesthetic reasons; Tree Service Pros has the equipment and experience. Call us for low cost tree removal services.

From massive oaks to small shrubs, there’s no job too big or too small. Our crews have over 100 years of combined experience in tree removal. It's no easy task, very dangerous and best left to professionals who utilize proper training and safety standards.

When is elimination necessary?

If you have a tree that’s diseased, it’s important to have it removed as soon as possible so the disease doesn’t spread.

If you have a tree that’s dead or dying, it can pose a threat to your home or property.

If a tree is causing an obstruction that is impossible to correct through pruning, removal may be the only option.

If a tree is crowding and causing harm to other trees, it’s better to lose one so the others will be healthy.

If a tree is becoming a hazard by growing too close to your home or building and causing structural damage, like cracks in the foundation.

Experienced tree removal company

Our experienced climbers are skilled in the removal of all sized trees, which can be a difficult and dangerous job. Situations where there is limited access, trees with extreme decay or those near utility lines can pose extra challenges, which is why a Tree service pros should only be done by professionals.

Safety is a priority

Our highly trained crew will make sure your home is safe and protected when removing your tree. We’ll carefully dismantle your tree and remove it piece by piece to ensure minimal damage to the surrounding area

In addition, all dangerous limbs and trunks are roped and slowly lowered to the ground once the cut has been made.

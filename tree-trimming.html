TREE TRIMMING


Pruning trees can reduce risk of storm damage to your home.

Can you see some sky through your trees?

When the canopy of your trees is dense, the wind can’t blow through them and that wind resistance causes them to break or even bring down the entire tree. Call us for tree trimming service.

All it takes is one of our famous Idaho storms to blow in and put your home and family in danger from falling branches. You can see there are more reasons to trim your trees than to simply keep them looking nice.

Whether the trees around your home are growing out of control and becoming a safety issue or you just want to keep them healthy, Tree Service Pros offers all kinds of tree pruning services to the Treasure Valley, from Caldwell to Eagle to Meridian.

From canopy cleaning to thin or raise the crown, removing dead or diseased branches, to shaping and fine shaping; we will keep your trees looking their best. Our crews are experienced in all types of pruning for both maintenance and hazard reduction safety.

If you are looking to have your trees trimmed or cut back, give us a call to schedule your free, no-obligation estimate. We’ll tell you what needs to be done to help keep your trees in the best shape possible.

Why do healthy trees need pruning?

With skilled tree care, your healthy trees will remain healthy longer. Proper trimmed trees allow more air to flow through the canopy, which is beneficial since resistance to wind can cause limbs to break or possibly topple the entire tree.

In other trees, thinning branches lowers the overall humidity, which can help prevent fungal infections on the leaves and stems.

Trimming not the same for all trees

It’s important to have a knowledgeable arborist working with your trees since individual tree species have different needs.

Some trees respond well to more aggressive trimming, while other trees require a gentle touch. With our more than 100 years of combined experience in the field of arboriculture, we know the best methods to ensure your trees live longer when trimmed properly.

Fruit tree pruning

We are experts on all fruit tree pruning, including ornamental, fruit bearing and any other NW species. As with all trees, regular sniping is needed for your apple, pear, cherry and other fruit trees to stay healthy.

Likewise, not all fruit trees are the same so only a certified arborist knows which methods to use so your trees will not be damaged and keep producing fruit year after year.

Shrub and hedge trimming

Regular trimming and pruning of your hedge or shrubs keeps them in control, healthy and looking nice. We trim all types of hedges: big, small, round, square, pillars, globes, topiaries, etc.

Not shaving or shearing on a regular basis may cause them to grow in with uncontrolled shape.

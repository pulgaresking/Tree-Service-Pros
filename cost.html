COST


How Much Do Tree Services Cost?

Since 1863, a forest has taken over the sagebrush of the Treasure Valley. Tree care companies stay busy year round by pruning, cutting and even removing dying ones to make room for new growth. Costs vary for tree services, lets break it down:

How Much Does It Cost to Remove a Tree?

Taking down a tree in will cost residents between $498 and $806, with an average quote of $622 for the job. This does not take into account the tree being near power lines or its proximity to the home. If either of these could be a problem, the cost will go up. The larger the tree diameter and the more limbs increases tree removal pricing. Learn more aboutTree Service Pros tree removal services.

How Much Does Stump Removal Cost?

Stump removal will cost around $250 to $300 and will take about three hours as they try to get the stump up from its roots and then haul it away. We offer stump removal services with affordable pricing. There is also the option to have the stump grind down into wood chips, which cost on the lower end of the spectrum. Contact us for a free quote.

Average Cost of Tree Maintenance

Depending on the number of trees and the species in a resident’s backyard, tree services professionals will quote a price somewhere in the range of $75 to $140 for tree maintenance on a single tree, with an average quote of $108. Charges are usually per visit with pesticide spraying and or fertilizing. Trimming or removing dead branches may be included.
